Grandstream 32 Port FXS Gateway

Description

Grandstream 32 FXS port gateway GXW4032 will enable any firm to build a cost-effective hybrid IP and analogue telephone system that will enable them to take advantage of VoIP communications while protecting their investment in current analogue phones, Fax machines, and legacy PBX systems. The GXW42 32 are completely SIP standard compliant and work with a variety of VoIP systems, analogue PBXs, and phones available today.
It has numerous FXS analogue phone ports, excellent voice quality, a variety of telephony capabilities, simple provisioning, customizable dialling plans, cutting-edge security protection, and a potent ability to manage a high volume of voice calls.
Businesses may take advantage of VoIP communications thanks to the GXW42XX series gateways, which provide a reasonably priced hybrid IP and analogue telephone system.
